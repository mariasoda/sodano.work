This infographic allowed me to revisit and analyze some of my favorite music videos and their relationships. One thing I noticed is I repeated videos less frequently than I thought, watching a total of 76 different music videos over six months. I hope that someone who spends time with this infographic can relate to some of the content or use some of the properties to find and explore something new.
