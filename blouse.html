Blouse is a magazine for career focused young women. Unlike other women's magazines, Blouse values its readers by cutting out misogynistic ideas and focusing on smart content. Blouse is strictly for women by women to achieve this. Articles span many topics, but they all focus on evaluating or advancing the position of women in business and society. This issue is focused on technology, with two featured articles and an interview.
