safe//incident
This book explores the relationship between safe spaces and incidents of street harassment using personal experiences. Each point is plotted with an embroidered knot on a map of text between the locations. The knots can be seen through the pages, representing the lasting impact of these experiences. The maps are prefaced by contextual information and followed by a written reflection. Photography by Ryan Wong
